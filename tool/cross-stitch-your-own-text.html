List of special characters:
'~' : 1pt space ~
'_' : 20pt of cross stitches _
'$' : cuts a ligature, including kerning (i.e when the kerning feature introduces a space between two consecutive characters, inserting a '$' between will remove that space).
Words have only one layout except with the rock'n roll font ('Festive'). Inserting one or more '$' at the begining of words may change their layout. Selects the 'Normal' font to see the special characters of your text.

The +/- buttons allows you to select the number of modifiable text areas, their width and the font size. The height of the text areas can be resized moving their bottom-right corner.

Notice that the fonts are incomplete and use contextual 'TrueType' features for ligatures that can be not fully supported by some web browsers.

It would be a shame to cross-stitch a text with a spelling mistake! So, a right click into a modifiable text area can be used to turn on/off the spell checker, select the checked language or add dictionaries from a menu set by the browser (these features are managed by your browser; the cross-stitch tool has nothing to do with them).
